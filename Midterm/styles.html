Style guide

Layout

I felt like the layout and aesthetic of speech bubbles is easy to recognize and would best picture how there are people who simply wants to speak/rant about the pressure of gender norms from both sexes. The content will be different for both genders and try to denounce gender roles.

Animations

I plan to go for smooth transitions/animations that are normally seen when texting: text bubble popping/fading in as you scroll down and, depending on the text, have some quirky animation that relates to the emotion. I also wish to have a little fade out on the elements that are out of focus.
