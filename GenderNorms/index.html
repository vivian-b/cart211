Are gender norms really that bad?

Yes. Even worse when you don’t conform to any of them in the first place.

How so?

Well, gender stereotypes or norms affects everyone because we are “assignned” one and are expected to follow some of its roles or character.

Even as a child, we are expected to behave like every other boy or every other girl. We become the odd one out if we do not act like the rest based on what we were born with.

But what happens when you do not identify to any of the genders in the first place? There should not be a trait that is applied to every peson of the same gender.

The same way there should not be a gender applied to every person, since some of us do not really identify ourselves with the biological gender we we’re born with.
